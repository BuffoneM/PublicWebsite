About

Who am I and what do I do?




Hello! I'm Michael Buffone and I'm a third year computer science student at Algoma University. I've been programming since 10th grade and have developed an enourmous passion for software development over the past five years. The indefinite amount of information to learn regarding programming is what keeps my motive high when developing software, as well as knowing that a tool I created is making someone else's job easier.

I've been sufficiently exposed to Linux, MacOS, and Windows environments for development roughly since the end of high school, along with using Github. The majority of my repositories are private due to the content being course material, however I plan on uploading projects soon.

For some additional information regarding how this portfolio is hosted, it's running on a virtual private server using Ubuntu with Nginx installed. The portfolio is also certified using Certbot to allow end-to-end encryption, and uses PHP with Postfix as an MTA (mail transfer agent) to send mail from the contact form to my desired address.
